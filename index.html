About

What is WomanHackWork

WomanHackWork is an empowerment network connecting like-minded peers anonymously to seek career advice when needed
. WomanHackWork believes that a supporting environment can help women acquire the skills to identify toxic work conditions and to resolve them to further advance their careers or to find the opportunities that are right for them

How to get started

Create a User Account
User names should not be associated with you name, last name or combination
Confirm your account
Submit an issue or question to the WomanHackWork network and select the hashtags from the suggested options that is more appropriate to your situation or create a new hashtag. Employer names or individuals referenced in your personal challenge should be omitted
Review posts from WomanHackWork network
Comment, Upvote or Report
Take charge of your career by selecting the right action for your style and/or situation

Code of Conduct

WomanHackWork is meant to be a safe environment where sensitive and difficult issues are shared. WomanHackWork expects everyone to abide by this code of conduct. Violation of this code could result in being banned from the WomanHackWork network and no longer have access to associated benefits
WomanHackWork doesn't tolerate discrimination of any kind, especially on the basis of race, ethnicity, religion, sexual orientation, national origin, disability, gender identity or citizenship status
WomanHackWork is committed to create a non-hostile space.
Respect one another
Listen with the intention to understand
. Help when you have something positive and constructive to share
Make WomanHackWork a comfortable environment based on your personal standard
Don't divulge our dialogues
Report any violations to the code of conduct
Report empowerment moments, raises and promotions
